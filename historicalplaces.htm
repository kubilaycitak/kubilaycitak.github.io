Historical Places of Eskisehir

Eskisehir has a history that goes to centuries ago, and there are still some historical places that you can visit. Travelling is not a problem at all, you can visit almost every place by public transportation. There are some examples:

Odunpazari Houses

Odunpazarı Houses are in the area of oldest settlement of Eskisehir and they are dated back to the Ottoman period.

Midas Monument

This monument might be the oldest historical place you can visit.

Seyyid Battal Gazi Complex

Battal Gazi Complex is a center for religious significance and it covers mosques, tombs etc.

Haci Hafiz Ahmet Efendi Konagi

A nice place that you can smell history in the air.

Yunus Emre Tomb

The grave of a religious poet, a great visiting place for your religious side.

Kursunlu Mosque

Also a religious place if you would like to stop by.
