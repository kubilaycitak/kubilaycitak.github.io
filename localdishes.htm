Local Dishes of Eskisehir

Beyond the fact that Eskisehir provides great opportunities for every kind of person and is a great city which grows day by day, the city also has a great culture of dishes. Every dish here has a famous restaourant that is identified with, a little research about the dish you want to have would be helpful enough. Here are some examples for that:

Balaban Kebab

It's a simple meal but one of the bests you can have.

Ciborek

Fried dough that contains minced meat, great meal.

Hashasli Gozleme

Light and filling meal, perfect if you are not that hungry.

Met Helvasi

A light dessert which has lots of different kinds.
